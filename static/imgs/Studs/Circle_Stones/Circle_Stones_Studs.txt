CIRCLE STUDS W/ STONES
 
These little circle studs are lightweight and perfect for everyday wear. They are a timeless and simple stud with an added gemstone creating a little more elegance from the traditional Circle Stud. The wire is round and the stone has been added using a wire wrapped technique. Each unique pair is available in 3 different sizes, 7mm, 9mm, 12mm in diameter.
 
Due to availability, any orders with gemstones will be placed by choice of color vs specific gemstone name.
 
Available in sterling silver or 14k yellow gold filled.
 
All Hand Twist Jewelry is handcrafted by me in the Columbus, OH area.
