TEARDROP STUDS W/ STONE
 
Simple and sophisticated teardrop earrings with a small charming gemstone. These easy to wear studs go nicely with many necklaces, and will add a quiet elegance to any outfit. The wire is round and the stone has been added using a wire wrapped technique. Teardrop measures approximately 12mm long, and 8mm wide.
 
Due to availability, any orders with gemstones will be placed by choice of color vs specific gemstone name.
 
Available in sterling silver or 14k yellow gold filled.
